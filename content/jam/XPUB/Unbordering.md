It is interesting to see how my perception of <q>border</q> changes depending of the language I think of it in.

Disability can be transient and related to age, accidents, transitory conditions… Fighting ableism is not about fixed categories and standards, but embracing acceptance of ephemeral problematic issues too.

- Real-life RPG as a transformative, non-formal, gossipy way of re-conceptualising social spaces?
- The border between cities and rural areas is (has to be?) blurred, same as the incompatibility between new and old devices must be flattened. Investigating the blurring of borders not only in physical sense, but also in conceptual and crafty sense.

Exploring the relationship and interconnection of **rhizomes**, **borders**, **hybridity**, **solarpunk**, **permacomputing**, and **Perma-Hybridity**.
