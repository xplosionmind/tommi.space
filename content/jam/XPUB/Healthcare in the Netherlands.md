As [I started working here](KvK.md) alongside [my studies](Tales%20from%20XPUB.md), I have to look for health insurance beyond AON, the basic and most common one, which only covers non-working students.

## Requirements

The only thing I need beyond basic health coverage is hopefully having my insurance cover therapy and, potentially, ADHD medication.

### “Ethical” insurance

As a proud [Banca Etica](https://bancaetica.it) member, I strongly prefer to choose providers that are cooperatives, and as ethical as possible. Someone pointed me in the direction of [Menzis](https://www.menzis.nl/englishwebsite/ 'Health insurer Menzis: for your health insurance') and [VGZ](https://www.vgz.nl/english 'VGZ Healthinsurance'). This mostly follows the little information I could gather, from these two sources:

- <em lang=nl>[Kies de juiste verzekeraar – Geen geld naar bezetting of wapens](https://kieszuiver.nl/zorgverzekering/)</em>
- <em lang=nl>[Beleidsscores – eerlijkegeldwijzer.n](https://www.eerlijkegeldwijzer.nl/verzekeringswijzer/beleidsscores)</em>

## Choosing a provider

[Barbs](https://gamedev.lgbt/@barbs '@barbs@gamedev.lgbt') kindly suggested that it is important to check that the chosen insurance has a contract with my therapist, GP, etc.

In the end, VGZ was the provider that overall seemed to suit my needs, even I did not do any in-depth research.
