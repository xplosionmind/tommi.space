While chatting with [Hannah](https://hannahaubry.online/ 'Hannah’s workshop') at the Mastodon booth at FOSDEM, I got an idea that brought together epiphanies and reflections I had in the past months, as I have started developing the feeling of wanting to go beyond Knitting Our Internet.

Inspired by George Perec’s <cite>[The Art of Asking Your Boss for a Raise](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Art_of_Asking_Your_Boss_for_a_Raise)</cite>, my idea is to make an interactive flowchart of a pseudo-ironic journey while introducing the Fediverse to a friend or a relative.
