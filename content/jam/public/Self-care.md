In 2026, my focus is going to be *self-care*. I feel trapped by the volatility and voracity of this epoch and this world. I want to slow down and learn to listen, to care for, and to love myself.

I have always seen caring for myself through the lens of achievements, problems to fix, relationships to cherish. I now see that true value starts inwards, from myself. I must question and explore my identity, let it flourish, and ultimately truly believe in who I am—or embrace a joyful and conscious rejection of any identity.

Not only I have to pursue this challenge, I also have to deconstruct years of venomous mechanisms that I involuntarily built and that now hinder my appreciation and care toward myself.

This presentment has been floating in my head already since 2025, especially at the beginning of [XPUB](Tales%20from%20XPUB.md), when I noted about the need of…

> **Rebalancing**: what is actually important, what is *necessary*, and what *is not*? Or better: what do I actually *want* to prioritise? What do I *care* about, what is actually good for me and what is good for the world?
>
> Fighting the FOMO and the performativity of wanting to do <q>things</q> just for the sake of it.
>
> Continues in [Ambizione](Ambizione.md)

Ultimately, I want to feel what it would be like to truly **love myself**, because I cannot sincerely say that I do right now.
