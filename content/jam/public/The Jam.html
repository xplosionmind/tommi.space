{% renderTemplate 'md' %} I happen to make curious discoveries, as well as I end up having interesting thoughts which I need to save before they end up forgotten or while I find the right words to reformulate them and place them in the [Zibaldone](/zibenglish). I take all of these things and I throw them here. ## What is The Jam In the wild, this is called [Digital Garden](https://dev.to/jbranchaud/the-digital-garden-l10 'The Digital Garden on DEV.to'), Zettelkasten system. In my case, it is a definitely less ambitious experiment: it is mixed, heterogeneous, juicy, and sweet. I call it The Jam. From this page, it is possible to start navigating in The Jam. There are two different ways to do this: - by choosing a tag - by opening one of the macro-notes (or MOCs) below, and diving deeper and deeper in growingly branched notes. {% endrenderTemplate %}
