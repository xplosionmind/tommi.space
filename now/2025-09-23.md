## ğŸ§ Guest Residency at Foundation B.a.d

In the South of Rotterdam there is a very special neighbourhood, called [Charlois](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charlois 'Charlois on Wikipedia'). It has a particular history, and a very vibrant art scene. It is like this also thanks to a group of art students who squatted an abandoned school in 1987, turning the classrooms in their studios and homes.

This special place is now <cite>[**Foundation B.a.d**](https://www.foundationbad.nl/)</cite>, and I am so glad I have been selected for [a guest residency](https://www.foundationbad.nl/guest-studios/) there.

As the main project for my residency, I want to host a serie of screenings about homelessness, precariety, and basically how **Capitalism and gentrification are devastating lives and increasing inequality**. I would love to explore this topic through fiction and non-fiction films, also with my fellow resident [Ariela Bergman](https://artoffice.info/kunstenaar/ariela-bergman/ 'Ariela Bergman on ArtOffice.info'). We are very open for inputs, considerations, and ideas! [More information here](/bad-rent/).
